Grilled Mojo-Marinated Skirt Steak Recipe

Calories / Serving: 782

Fat: 65g

Carbs: 6g

Protein: 46g

Ingredients:
  • 2 pounds skirt steak, trimmed of excess fat (see note above)
  • 2 tbs of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup juice from 1 orange
  • 2/3 cup olive oil, divided
  • 4 medium cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Old Fashioned

Glass: Old-Fashioned Glass

Ingredients:

  • 4.5 cL Bourbon
  • 2 dashes Angostura
  • 1 cube Sugar
  • 1 dash water

Instructions:

  1. Place sugar cube in old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters, add a dash of plain water.
  2. Muddle until dissolved.
  3. Fill the glass with ice cubes and add whiskey.
  4. Garnish with orange twist, and a cocktail cherry.
